About

Since 1959, ask any Linda's Bakery customer how fresh our breads cakes and pastries are, and you will hear only one response... Today Fresh!

 

It all started nearly six decades ago when Mr. Lionel Chin Fatt saw the need for high quality baked goods at an affordable price, so he opened Linda’s Bakery, proudly named in tribute to his wife and as a guarantee to his customers that their products would always be worthy of her name.

 

Fast forward to the present, today. Linda's products are a household favourite and our breads, cakes, pastries and ever-evolving range of delicious breakfast, lunch and snack items are still always Today Fresh!

We pride ourselves on being a progressive, creative bakery that experiments with innovation and international trends, in an effort to satisfy a variety of tastes without compromising quality or value.

We're your local, neighbourhood bakery and look forward to serving you fresh, wholesome food everyday!
